About

My aunt lost her job during COVID and was unemployed for close to a year. She experienced financial constraints and frustrations from costantly getting rejection emails from job applications. A video of a young woman sharing how she started a chapati business with 5 dollars changed her life. My aunt is an excellent cook and within two weeks of cooking and selling chapatis, the entire neighbourhood were scrambling to get their orders. Two years later, my aunt delivers pastries across the city and runs her own business venture that has more than 15 employees. This is what inspired Biz. This is the platform to get you started on your entrepreneurship venture. Join the Trail.
